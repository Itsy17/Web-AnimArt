Privacy and Policy

Introduction

At AnimArt, we value your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our website and commission services.

Information We Collect

We may collect the following information:
-Account Information: Name, email address, username, and password.
-Transaction Data: Payment details, billing information, and commission history.
-Communication: Messages exchanged between clients and artists.
-Usage Data: Website activity, preferences, and technical information (IP address, browser type).

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:
-Facilitate commission requests between clients and artists.
-Process payments securely.
-Provide customer support and resolve disputes.
-Improve website functionality and user experience.
-Send important updates about your account or our services.

Data Sharing

-We do not sell your personal information.
-Data may be shared with trusted third-party services (e.g., payment processors) only as necessary.
-Artists and clients only see the information required to complete a commission.

Security

-We use encryption and secure servers to protect your data.
-While we strive to safeguard your information, no system is 100% secure.

Your Rights

You have the right to:
-Access and update your personal information.
-Request deletion of your account and associated data.
-Opt out of non-essential communications.

Cookies and Tracking

-AnimArt uses cookies to enhance user experience and remember preferences.
-You may disable cookies in your browser, but some features may not function properly.

Children's Privacy

AnimArt is not intended for users under 13 years old. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated effective date.
